About Me

I am a recent graduate (2019) from Case Western Reserve University and the Cleveland Institute of Music, BA in Mathematics and BM in Viola Performance. After spending some time working for NWPS as an Analyst, I am seeking a position more closely aligned with my interests in technology and research.

My dream is to research the science of music, and would particularly enjoy pushing the boundaries of music technology and software. To that end, I'm looking for employment as a coding professional.

I worked with the Virtual Learning department at CIM to present music lessons with current technology--from history to science applications--to audiences anywhere between kindergarten and senior retirement around the globe. Reaching out to disparate communities in this way illuminates both the importance of music to all human beings, and the importance of technology that allows us to reach disparate communities.
